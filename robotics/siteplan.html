Project Siteplan

Site Name

Mammoth Mechanics

The name chosen for this website is the name of the robotics team that I am promoting.

Site Purpose

My website is going to promote a robotics team. It will have the team members, our achievments, upcoming events, a form to give sponsership and a form for joining the team.

Scenarios

How much money does it take to build your robot? Is your team through a school? What times do you meet?

Color Schema

#FF6F00

is the background color of the header and the footer.

black

is the color of the text for the header, main and footer.

rgb(77, 76, 76)

is the hover background color and side border for the li elements.

Typograghy

The Lora font is used for the header h1, the h2, the h3 and the a tags. The Roboto font is used for the paragraphs and the footer and the Agbalumo font is used for the h1 element in the main tags.
