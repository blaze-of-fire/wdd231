Project Siteplan

Site Name

Mammoth Mechanics

The name chosen for this website is the name of the robotics team that I am promoting.

Site Purpose

My website is going to promote a robotics team. How their robot is doing and what challenge they are trying to build the robot for. along with what the robot looks like in a timeline the teams past and future events, a form to give sponsership and a form for joining the team.

Scenarios

How much money does it take to build your robot? Is your team through a school? What times do you meet?

Color Schema

#FF6F00

is the background color of the header and the footer.

black

is the color of the text for the header, main and footer.

white

is for the background for the main content.

Typograghy

The Lora font is used for h1, h2, h3 and the a tags. The Roboto font is used for the paragraphs and the footer.
